About US

Initiated by Eng. Wickremasinghe as an individual entrepreneuring  venture  in 1999,  Pacific Hydo Electric (PHE) was incorporated in 2010 , as an Engineering Company with Strong Focus on Renewable Energy.

Commencing its voyage towards renewable energy by discovering and co developing new hydro power projects and broadening its business segments from hydro to solar, biomass, wind power and energy investment management, PHE has widened its capacity having experience in the renewable energy sector for more than two decades, holding investment arms for renewable energy projects as well.

In 2018 PHE sailed towards international waters  by making its presence in Sub-Saharan Africa. With a vision of becoming a globally recognized brand in the renewable energy sector, PHE with its passionate and dedicated team strives towards putting Sri Lanka in the global map.

Eng. Prabath Wickremasinghe

Managing Director

Starting his carrier as an Electrical Engineer graduating from University of Peradeniya in 1999, Prabath has a cumulative experience in renewable energy sector for more than 20 years.He also holds Management Qualifications from Chartered Institute of Management Accountancy (CIMA) of UK (1993-1998) & qualified in Marketing from Chartered Institute of Marketing UK (1993-1998). His experience extends to the development and marketing renewable energy projects. Prabath has also extended his ventures internationally to Sub-Saharan Africa. He has been involved the in development, planning, construction and commissioning and operating more than 25 utility scale renewable power plants which includes large number of small hydropower (75 MW cumulative capacity), wind, solar and bio-mass power plants.

Eng. Lakmal Ekanayaka

General Manager

Lakmal is a Chartered Civil Engineer having graduated from University of Peradeniya in 2000. He is a member of Institute of Engineers Sri Lanka and Institute of Structural Engineers Sri Lanka. He holds a Masters in Business Administration from Cardiff Metropolitan University. He also has a Post Graduate Diploma in Construction Management.
He has more than 20 years of local and overseas experience in renewable energy projects, construction management, project planning and business management and administration.

Eng. Udara Priyankara

Head of Operation and Maintenance

An electrical engineer by profession, Udara is a graduate from University of Peradeniya specialized in Power and Telecommunication Engineering. He is an associate member of Institute of engineers Sri Lanka. He has over 12 years of experience in small Hydro power sector in electro-mechanical machine selection and equipment installation, IoT solutions for power plant automation, testing, commissioning, operation and maintenance.

Eng. Gayan Prabuddha

Prabuddha is an Electrical Engineer by profession having graduated from University of Moratuwa. He holds a Post graduate Diploma in Electrical Engineering from University of Moratuwa. He is also as Associate Member of CIMA - UK and a Chartered Global Management Accountant of AICPA.
